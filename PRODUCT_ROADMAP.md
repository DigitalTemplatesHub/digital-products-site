# Digital Products Hub Product Roadmap

## Immediate Goal
Build a small but focused catalog of high-demand digital products that can be promoted through SEO blog posts, a free lead magnet, and email automation.

## Product Strategy
Instead of selling generic templates, each product should target a specific buyer and solve a clear problem. Specific products are easier to market, easier to write blog content for, and easier to bundle later.

## Phase 1: First Sellable Products

### Freelancer Budget Spreadsheet Toolkit
**Buyer:** Freelancers, gig workers, creators, and small service businesses.

**Problem solved:** Helps track income, expenses, taxes, savings goals, and inconsistent monthly cash flow.

**Included files:**
- Monthly income tracker
- Expense tracker
- Tax set-aside calculator
- Savings goal tracker
- Example-filled version
- Quick-start PDF

**Suggested price:** $9–19.

**Upsell potential:** Bundle with a freelancer finance ebook or a quarterly business review template.

### Notion Productivity Command Center
**Buyer:** Creators, students, solopreneurs, and people managing multiple projects.

**Problem solved:** Gives buyers one clean dashboard for goals, habits, tasks, content ideas, and weekly planning.

**Included files:**
- Notion dashboard template
- Weekly planner
- Habit tracker
- Content calendar
- Project tracker
- Setup guide

**Suggested price:** $19–49.

**Upsell potential:** Add niche versions for creators, streamers, students, freelancers, and small businesses.

### AI Skills Starter Guide for Non-Tech Professionals
**Buyer:** Beginners, small business owners, marketers, writers, creators, and job seekers.

**Problem solved:** Helps people understand practical AI use without coding.

**Included files:**
- PDF ebook
- Prompt examples
- AI workflow checklist
- Beginner tool list
- Quick-start exercises

**Suggested price:** $9–29.

**Upsell potential:** Create a prompt pack, video course, or workshop later.

### Resume & Cover Letter Template Pack
**Buyer:** Job seekers who want professional-looking materials quickly.

**Problem solved:** Saves time and improves presentation without hiring a designer.

**Included files:**
- Resume template
- Cover letter template
- Reference page template
- Google Docs version
- Word version
- Editing guide

**Suggested price:** $7–19.

**Upsell potential:** Make industry-specific packs for customer service, security, music, teaching, remote jobs, tech, and creative roles.

### Social Media Content Calendar Pack
**Buyer:** Small businesses, streamers, event promoters, creators, and local brands.

**Problem solved:** Helps buyers plan consistent posts without guessing what to publish.

**Included files:**
- 30-day content calendar
- Caption prompt sheet
- Hashtag planning worksheet
- Reel idea list
- Promo launch checklist

**Suggested price:** $9–29.

**Upsell potential:** Bundle with Canva social media templates and niche versions for events, restaurants, streamers, and service businesses.

## Phase 2: Free Lead Magnet
Create a free downloadable checklist to collect email subscribers.

**Lead magnet name:** Digital Product Launch Starter Kit

**Includes:**
- Product idea validation checklist
- Buyer avatar worksheet
- Simple pricing worksheet
- Launch checklist
- Email welcome sequence preview

## Phase 3: Blog Content Plan
Use SEO blog posts to attract buyers and push them toward the free lead magnet and paid products.

### Blog Topics
1. How to Choose a Profitable Digital Product Idea
2. Best Digital Products for Freelancers to Sell Online
3. How to Build a Simple Digital Product Launch Plan
4. Notion Templates vs Spreadsheets: Which Should You Sell?
5. How to Price a Digital Product Without Guessing
6. Digital Product Ideas for Creators and Streamers
7. How to Turn Your Skills Into a Sellable Digital Product
8. Free Digital Product Launch Checklist

## Phase 4: Email Automation
Set up a simple welcome flow after someone downloads the free starter kit.

### Email Sequence
1. Deliver the free kit and introduce the brand.
2. Teach how to pick one product idea.
3. Explain why specific niches sell better than generic products.
4. Recommend the first paid product.
5. Offer a limited-time bundle or discount.

## Phase 5: Website Updates
Add the following pages to the site:

- Free Starter Kit landing page
- Product detail page for each first product
- Email signup section connected to a real email platform
- Blog posts tied to each product category
- About page with a stronger founder-style brand story

## Priority Order
1. Create the free lead magnet.
2. Add a landing page for the lead magnet.
3. Choose and build the first paid product.
4. Set up email automation.
5. Publish supporting blog posts.
6. Launch product pages and begin promoting.
