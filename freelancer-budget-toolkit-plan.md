# Freelancer Budget Spreadsheet Toolkit – Product Plan

## Overview

The **Freelancer Budget Spreadsheet Toolkit** is a comprehensive, easy‑to‑use spreadsheet solution designed to help freelancers and solopreneurs take control of their finances. It consolidates income tracking, expense categorization, tax estimation and goal planning into one clear, customizable workbook. The toolkit is delivered as a fully editable Microsoft Excel/Google Sheets file with formulas and instructions built in. It’s designed for creatives, consultants, digital product sellers, and other independent professionals who need a simple, reliable way to manage money without expensive bookkeeping software.

## Target Buyer

- **Freelancers & Contractors** — designers, developers, writers, consultants and other self‑employed individuals who invoice clients and track project income/expenses.
- **Solopreneurs & Side‑hustlers** — small business owners running one‑person online shops or services who need a clear view of cash flow.
- **Digital Creators** — Etsy sellers, bloggers, YouTubers and course creators who juggle multiple revenue streams and want to forecast earnings.
- **Students & New Entrepreneurs** — anyone new to self‑employment who needs a starter tool for budgeting and tax prep.

## What’s Included

The toolkit contains several interconnected sheets with built‑in formulas and simple dashboards. Key components:

| Sheet | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Income Tracker** | Record all invoices or sales by date, client/product, category and payment method. Automatically sums monthly and yearly earnings. |
| **Expense Tracker** | Log expenses with category drop‑downs (office supplies, software, marketing, travel, taxes, etc.). Calculates totals and highlights tax‑deductible items. |
| **Profit & Loss Summary** | Consolidates income and expense data to show monthly profit, year‑to‑date totals, and net margin. Includes charts for visual insights. |
| **Tax Estimator** | Applies self‑employment tax rates and estimated quarterly taxes based on income and deductible expenses. Auto‑updates when data changes. |
| **Savings & Goal Planner** | Helps set financial goals (emergency fund, retirement, equipment upgrade, etc.) and tracks progress toward each goal. |
| **Invoice Log & Number Generator** | Keeps a sequential record of invoice numbers and due dates. Generates next invoice number automatically. |
| **Dashboard** | A high‑level view summarizing earnings, expenses, top clients/categories, upcoming tax obligations and goal progress using charts and conditional formatting. |
| **Instructions & Example Data** | A separate sheet explaining how to use each tab, customise categories, and a sample data set to experiment with before entering real numbers. |

## Pricing Strategy

- **Base Price**: $12 USD – affordable enough for freelancers while reflecting the value of an all‑in‑one toolkit.
- **Launch Promotion**: 25% off (priced at $9) for the first two weeks to incentivise early buyers and gather testimonials.
- **Bundle Upsell**: Offer as part of a “Productivity Power Pack” bundle with the Notion Productivity Command Center and Resume & Cover Letter Template Pack for $29 ($45 value). Bundling increases average order value and encourages cross‑sales.
- **Licensing**: Single‑use personal license; customers may duplicate and modify for their own business but cannot resell.
